## FAQ Copy

**What types of content can I include in experiences?**
> Spatial Studio supports text, images, audio, video, 360° media, AR objects, and interactive assessments. Content can be triggered by location (geofence, GPS), proximity (Bluetooth beacons), time, or user progress.

**Do I need a developer to create experiences?**
> No. Studio is designed for content creators, educators, and program managers. Our visual interface lets you build and publish experiences without writing code. For advanced integrations, our API is available.

**Can I white-label experiences for my organization?**
> Yes. Experiences can be deployed under your brand through our white-label options. This includes custom apps, embedded experiences on your website, or branded versions of our universal player.

**How do end users access experiences?**
> Users access experiences through your app, your website, or our universal player web app. No special hardware required—just a smartphone with location services.

**What analytics are available?**
> Track session starts, completions, time spent, content interactions, pathway choices, and assessment results. Data can be viewed in our dashboard or exported to your analytics tools.

**How does pricing work?**
> Pricing is based on your organization type, number of experiences, and expected usage. Contact us for a customized quote.
